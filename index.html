How to Play:

  • Goal: Find the maximum weighted matching
  • Edges: Click to highlight/unhighlight edges
  • Weights: Double click numbers to edit (-1 to 1)
  • Nodes: Drag to reposition
  • Sets: Red (A) and Blue (B) nodes
  • Matching: Can't share nodes
  • Score: Sum of selected edge pairs
  • Check: Verify if matching is optimal
